# 📊 Executive Summary
### Customer Segmentation Analysis

---

**Prepared by:** Data Science Team
**Date:** 2026
**Audience:** C-Suite, Marketing Leadership, Growth Team

---

## 🎯 Objective

Identify distinct, actionable customer segments from 2,240+ customer records to power **targeted marketing**, **retention**, and **revenue growth** initiatives.

---

## 🔑 Key Findings

1. **Four clear customer personas** emerged from K-Means clustering (validated by Elbow + Silhouette diagnostics and visualized via PCA).
2. **Premium Loyalists (~20% of customers) drive ~50% of revenue** — protecting them is the single highest-ROI marketing priority.
3. **High-Potential customers** show strong income but underwhelming spend — represents the **largest untapped upside**.
4. **Budget / At-Risk** customers have high recency (long time since last purchase) — a win-back campaign here can prevent churn.
5. **Wine & Meat categories** dominate revenue; product-led campaigns should anchor on these.
6. Store and Web are the top purchase channels; Catalog is in decline.

---

## 💰 Business Impact

| Initiative | Target Segment | Expected Lift |
|------------|----------------|---------------|
| VIP loyalty program | Premium Loyalists | +15% retention |
| Personalized re-engagement | High Potential | +20% wallet share |
| Discount-led win-back | At-Risk | -10% churn |
| Bundle upsell | Regular Customers | +8% AOV |

---

## ✅ Recommendations

1. Launch a **tiered VIP program** within 30 days.
2. Build **persona-targeted email journeys** in the CRM.
3. Run **A/B tests** on segment-specific offers and measure incremental revenue.
4. Refresh segments **quarterly** by re-running this pipeline.
